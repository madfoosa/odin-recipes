The Perfect PB&J

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly - Grape
  • Sandwich Bread

Assembly Order

  1. Get two pieces of bread and put them on a plate
  2. Apply the peanut butter to one piece of bread
  3. Apply Jelly tot he other piece of bread
  4. Put the two pieces of bread together
